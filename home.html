Exploring Colour Palette Usage in the Design of Data Visualizations

Consent Form

Researcher: Jarryullah Ahmad

Affiliation: Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto

Background and Purpose

You are invited to participate in a research study investigating color usage in creating data visualizations. This research involves a questionnaire that will evaluate your education level, level of familiarity with charts, and your ability to answer questions from data visualizations and an experiment involving selection of appropriate color palettes for certain visualizations.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate you must be in 18 years of age or older and be comfortable with communicating in English.

Voluntary Participation & Early Withdrawal

Your decision to participate is voluntary and you are free to withdraw at any time without any consequences. You are not required to answer any questions you do not feel comfortable answering.

Upon completion of the experiment, you will be given a participant ID to submit on the Amazon Mechanical Turk website. You can withdraw from the study either by not submitting your participant ID, or by emailing the researchers with your participant ID and requesting to withdraw. In both cases your data will be destroyed and not included in the published results of the research study. After results are made public, anonymized data may be shared publicly and you will no longer be able to withdraw from the study.

Confidentiality and Privacy

You will not be asked to share your name and participants will be referred to as a participant ID number only. During data collection, data will be stored on a secure, encrypted server. After completion of the experiment, data will be stored on an encrypted hard drive of a password-protected lab computer that is only accessible by the researcher.
